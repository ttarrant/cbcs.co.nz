Complete Bodycorp Solutions

Shayne Thurston (Director & Property Manager) and Ben Duflou (Director & Chartered Accountant) set up Complete Body Corporate Solutions Limited to provide exceptional body corporate management services.

Shayne and Ben had identified through their discussions with unit owners and body corporate committee members, that obtaining understandable and timely information was difficult to achieve. They also identified that body corporate committees generally struggled to fully appreciate and meet the numerous obligations under the Unit Titles Act.

Complete Body Corp Solutions provides a wide range of body corporate management and accounting services, including contract management of service providers, managing procedural issues, ensuring committee compliance with Unit Titles Act, issuing disclosure statements, monthly reporting, maintenance planning, taxation compliance and year-end reporting.
